Collegiate Scholar Link

A browser extension makes better use of Google Scholar for scholars.

Don't know how to download a scientific paper from a publisher off campus? Collegiate Scholar Link provides additional links for Google Scholar search results. Through those links, you can access the articles anywhere via your university's proxy service. If you're tired of VPN and poor searching functionality of your university's library portal, Collegiate Scholar Link may ease your life a little.
